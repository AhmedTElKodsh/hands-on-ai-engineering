## What This Is

This folder contains the **modified, pragmatic version** of the Layer 1 curriculum, designed for:
- **Ship-first learning** (working systems over perfect understanding)
- **GenAI Engineer focus** (RAG, Agents, LLM Apps - not ML research)
- **Guided discovery** (TODOs and hints, not complete solutions)
- **Real learning friction** (flex weeks, debugging labs, checkpoints)

## Philosophy

### Original Layer 1
> "Build first, understand after — but prime the pump with the right mental model before you touch code."

### Modified Curriculum
> "Build with guidance, explain to proceed. Working systems > perfect understanding. Ship, break, fix, repeat."

**Both are valid.** Choose based on your learning style:
- **Original:** Better for self-directed learners who want comprehensive coverage
- **Modified:** Better for those who need structure, checkpoints, and flex time
