## 🔥 WHY TRACK FAILURES?

**Purpose:**
- Normalize struggle as part of learning
- Build debugging resilience
- Create interview stories ("Tell me about a time you debugged...")
- Document what actually breaks in AI systems
- Show employers you can handle production incidents

**What to Track:**
- Every bug that took >15 minutes to fix
- Every architectural mistake
- Every production deployment failure
- Every "I thought I understood but didn't" moment

**Update Frequency:** Every Friday (Debug Lab day) — mandatory entry
