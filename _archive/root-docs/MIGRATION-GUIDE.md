## 💬 FAQ

### Q: Do I need to redo my projects?
**A:** No! Your projects transfer. Just add iteration versions (v1→v2→v3) and adopt LAYER1-FINAL structure.

### Q: Will I finish faster with 28 weeks vs. 32?
**A:** Yes, but the pace is similar. LAYER1-FINAL just has better sequencing and fewer flex weeks.

### Q: What if I prefer 4 flex weeks?
**A:** Use LAYER1-FINAL's 2 flex weeks + add 2 self-directed flex weeks at natural break points.

### Q: Is the specialization track mandatory?
**A:** Yes in LAYER1-FINAL. Week 16 has 3 structured tracks (NL2SQL, Doc Intelligence, Fine-Tuning).

### Q: Can I switch back to modified if I don't like LAYER1-FINAL?
**A:** You can, but don't. LAYER1-FINAL has correct content sequencing (SQL Week 1) and complete templates.
