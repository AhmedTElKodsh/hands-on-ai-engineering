3. ✅ Scaffolded projects with TODOs, not solutions
4. ✅ Progressive hints (3 levels)

### Lessons Learned
- Comprehensive documentation upfront saves time later
- Clear structure makes it easier to add content
- Teaching methodology is critical for AI assistants
- Checkpoints ensure understanding, not just completion
